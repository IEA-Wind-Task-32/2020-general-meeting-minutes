{\Large\noindent%
	Task 32 has created a worldwide network of wind lidar researchers who meet regularly to identify opportunities for the use of wind lidar, and mitigate the barriers to its adoption.
}
\vskip 6pt

The 2020 General Meeting took place online because of the COVID-19 pandemic.  COVID-19 made networking and collaboration harder during 2020, and so the 2020 General Meeting was designed to let people mingle virtually with their colleagues through a mix of discussion, working groups, and networking sessions.

\tableofcontents

The meeting was moderated by Andy Clifton, Task 32 Operating Agent. The working group sessions were run by David Schlipf (Task 32 Operating Agent) and many members of the Task 32 Advisory Board. The minutes were written by Ines Würth and then converted into this document by Andrew Clifton.
